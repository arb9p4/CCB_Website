Keith House Scholarship

To support excellence in the instrumental music tradition, The Columbia Community Band has created a scholarship fund in honor of former CCB music director Keith E. House. Each year, a deserving Mid-Missouri high school student intending to continue participation in a band related ensemble in college will receive the Keith E. House Scholarship.

Keith was a beloved teacher and conductor who spent part of his professional career at the Swinney Conservatory at Central Methodist University in Fayette, Missouri. Keith is remembered as an inspiring professional who shaped the careers of many top-notch musicians an public-school music directors. In doing so, Keith enhanced the quality of music education across the United States.

Please help us to reach our goal of $10,000 for a permanet scholarship fund. Your generous donation will honor Keith and help make this scholarship possible.
