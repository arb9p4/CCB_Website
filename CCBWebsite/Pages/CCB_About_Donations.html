The Columbia Community Band is funded through grants, individual and corporate donations.
Your contributions to the Band are Greatly Appreciated!

Friend $25
Supporter $50
Sustaining Member $100
Patron $500
Benefactor $1000

Donations can be directed to our general fund which helps sustain the Columbia Community Band or to our Keith House Memorial Scholarship Fund
