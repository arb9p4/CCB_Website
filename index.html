Welcome!

Welcome to the The Columbia Community Band website, home to one of the Midwest's premier symphonic concert bands. Founded in 1981, the band offers membership to professional and non-professional musicians from all across the Mid-Missouri community. Performances include classical, contemporary, and festive pieces in a wide variety of community venues.
